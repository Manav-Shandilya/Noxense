# Noxense - Expense Tracker

A simple and efficient expense tracker application designed to help you manage and monitor your spending habits. Track your expenses, categorize transactions, and gain insights into your financial patterns.

## Overview

Noxense is a web-based expense tracking application that allows users to record, categorize, and analyze their daily expenses. With an intuitive interface and powerful features, managing your finances has never been easier.

## Features

### 💰 Expense Management
- **Add Expenses**: Quickly record new expenses with details like amount, category, date, and description
- **View Expenses**: See all your recorded transactions in a clear, organized list
- **Edit Expenses**: Modify existing expense entries to correct or update information
- **Delete Expenses**: Remove unwanted transactions from your records

### 📊 Categories
- Pre-defined expense categories (Food, Transportation, Entertainment, Utilities, Shopping, etc.)
- Organize expenses by category for better tracking and analysis
- Visual category-based organization for easier budgeting

### 📈 Insights & Analytics
- View total expenses at a glance
- Category-wise expense breakdown
- Track spending patterns over time
- Identify areas where you can save money

### 💾 Data Persistence
- Local storage support to save your expenses
- Your data persists across browser sessions
- No need for account creation or login

### 🎨 User-Friendly Interface
- Clean and intuitive design
- Responsive layout that works on desktop and mobile devices
- Easy navigation and quick access to features

## How It Works

1. **Record an Expense**: Click the "Add Expense" button and fill in the expense details (amount, category, date, and description)
2. **View Your Expenses**: All expenses are displayed in a list format with key details visible at a glance
3. **Categorize**: Each expense is tagged with a category to help organize and analyze your spending
4. **Track & Analyze**: Monitor your total spending and see which categories consume the most money
5. **Manage Entries**: Edit or delete expenses as needed to keep your records accurate

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+)
- **Storage**: Browser Local Storage
- **Architecture**: Client-side single-page application
